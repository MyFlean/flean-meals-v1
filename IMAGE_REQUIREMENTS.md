## Design Guidelines

1. **Food Photography:**
   - Professional food styling
   - Natural lighting preferred
   - Clean, uncluttered backgrounds
   - Appetizing and fresh appearance
   - Consistent color grading across all images
   - High resolution (minimum 1200x1200px, preferably 2000x2000px)

2. **Kitchen Photography:**
   - Clean, modern commercial kitchens
   - Emphasis on hygiene and professionalism
   - Warm, inviting lighting
   - Organized and efficient appearance
   - High resolution (minimum 1200x1200px)

3. **Icons:**
   - Consistent design language
   - Simple, recognizable shapes
   - Vector format (SVG preferred)
   - Scalable to different sizes
   - Match the app's color palette (deep green, warm neutrals)
   - Modern, clean aesthetic

4. **Hero Icons:**
   - Larger and more detailed than regular icons
   - Eye-catching and prominent
   - Should work well on pastel backgrounds
   - Minimum 200x200px for vector, or high-res raster
